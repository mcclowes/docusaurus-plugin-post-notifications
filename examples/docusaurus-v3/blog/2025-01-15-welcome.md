Welcome to the new post toast plugin demo!

This blog post demonstrates the toast notification feature. When you visit this site, you'll see a toast notification alerting you to new blog posts.

<!-- truncate -->

## How It Works

The plugin tracks your last visit using localStorage and compares it against blog post dates. When you return to the site, it shows toast notifications for any posts published since your last visit.

## Features

- Customizable position (bottom-right, top-left, etc.)
- Auto-dismiss with configurable duration
- Dismiss individual toasts
- Remember dismissed posts
- Dark mode support
- Mobile responsive

Try refreshing the page or navigating between pages to see the toast in action!
